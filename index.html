Frequently Asked Questions

What is asset bundling?

Grouping assets together to reduce load requests and improve performance.

What is lazy loading?

Loading assets only when needed instead of upfront.

Why is loading performance important?

Slow loading can reduce player retention and experience quality.

How can I optimize rendering?

Reduce asset size, optimize pipelines, and load resources efficiently.

What affects loading speed?

Asset size, network latency, and inefficient pipelines.
